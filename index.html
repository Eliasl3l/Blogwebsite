He changed school, whhere alot of people smoked, which he wasn't used to, and the people there were less like him, especially the guys, they only cared about superficialities. He made an exchange year and came back. Everybody changed, even if it was just a little, and it was strange, because everybody thought they knew him and he thought so too. Nevertheless I do have some connections and friends, but they have weakened. It could just be another stay with a hostfamily, where I could get kicked out. Maybe the exchange year was a mistake.
